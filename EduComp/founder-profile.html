Joe Wagner is the founder of Harper School. Joe previously co-founded Stratford School, a high performing private preschool and elementary school in Silicon Valley.

Joe has degrees from Georgetown University and The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Joe grew up in St. Louis and currently lives in University City with his wife, Molly, and their seven children.

START
1. Visit or Inquire

A. Schedule a campus visit with or without your child. When you tour the campus, you will have the opportunity to ask questions and see what makes Harper unique.

B. When you visit, whether you apply to Harper or not, we will set up free online accounts for your child to try math and coding at Khan Academy and Code.org.

C. If you do not have time for a visit at this time, please call us at (314) 540-3290 to ask any questions you may have or simply proceed to Step 2 to fill out the online application.

2. Online Application

Fill out the online application and pay the $75 application fee.

3. Student Assessment (K-5 only)

If you are applying for Kindergarten or elementary grades first through fifth (K-5), we will contact you for a student assessment after you complete the online application.

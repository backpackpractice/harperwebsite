Preschool Overview

Harper instills a love for learning in your child.

Harper Preschool is a safe and nurturing environment that prepares students to be ready to succeed academically and socially in Kindergarten.

There is a strong emphasis on reading, phonics, math, and learning by doing (e.g. writing the alphabet, counting, simple coding, robotics, playing musical instruments).

Harper Programs

  • 5 yrs

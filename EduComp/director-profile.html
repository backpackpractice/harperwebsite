Staci Jerina is the Preschool Director of Harper School. Staci most recently served as Director of Summer Camps and Supervisor of Youth Programming for Eureka Parks and Recreation. Previously, she was a preschool teacher and summer camp director for Samuel Preschool in Clayton.

Staci graduated from Missouri State University with a BS in Psychology and minor in Child and Family Development.

Staci grew up in Kirkwood and currently lives in Eureka with her husband, Mike, and their three daughters.

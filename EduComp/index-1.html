Our Principles

01.

High Expectations. Kids excel when expextations are set high.

02.

Active Learning. Kids learn best by doing, by being actively engaged in learning.

03.

Mastery of three Rs (reading, writing, and arithmetic) is foundation of strong education.

04.

Parent involvement. Parents are their children's first and most important teachers.

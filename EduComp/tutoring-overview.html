Tutoring Overview

Harper provides tutoring in math, reading, study skills,test prep, and additional subjects on a case by case basis.

We offer three types of tutoring: 1) personalized one-on-one tutoring, 2) small group tutoring and practice, 3) supervised study and practice. Depending on the needs of your child, you can focus on one or more subjects by signing up for one-on-one tutoring or supplementing it with small group tutoring or supervised practice.

Harper utilized your child's curriculum (e.g. your child's math book or novel) but also makes use of personalized learning software (e.g. Accelerated Reader, Dreambox Learning, Khan Academy, ST Math). Our goal it make sure your child is making progress academically and gaining the confidence to master subject material independently.

Harper makes learning fun and challenging and instills "can do" attitude.

Our first goal is to make sure your child loves going to school, not only because it's fun and social but also because they enjoy the challenge of successfuly learning new things. This love of learning and confidence that "I can do it" will become the foundation for their success in Kindergarten and beyond."

Joe Wagner, Founder

Our second goal is to make sure we are instilling in your child the same character strengths and virtues that you as parents are working on at home. We want to help our kids to be not just good learners, but good kids too, kind and well-behaved.

Joe Wagner, Founder

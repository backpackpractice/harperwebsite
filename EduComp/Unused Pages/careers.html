Harper School is a private preschool and elementary school (K-5) located in Chesterfield, Missouri.

Harper provides your child with a personalized learning experience that is challenging and fun. We balance a rigorous academic program focused on traditional subjects with a strong emphasis on 21st century skills like computer coding.

We aim for academeic success in the classroom but also want to make sure our students are well-rounded and good kids. We aspire for our students to be both high achieving and high character.
